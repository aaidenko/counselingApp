Our Story

Being a teenager comes with a mix of emotions, challenges, and big questions about who you are and where you’re headed. We created this app to be a safe space where you can talk, reflect, and get the support you need—without fear of judgment.
‍
Meet Mungchi, our yarn ball mascot! Life can feel tangled sometimes, but with patience, support, and the right tools, those knots can be unraveled. We believe that no one should have to figure everything out alone.

This project was born from a simple goal: to make mental health support more accessible and welcoming for young people. Here, you can express yourself freely, connect with understanding people, and get guidance that’s just for you. Whatever you're going through, you’re not alone—we’re here to help!
