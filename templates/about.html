The Need for Change

Rising Mental Health Concern
Globally, 14% of youth aged 10–19 experience mental health challenges, according to the WHO.
Limited Access to Support
In South Korea, nearly 40% of young people recognize their need for mental health support but lack access to professional resources.
Emotional Support Deficits
Reports from the OECD highlight that South Korean youth rank among the lowest in life satisfaction, indicating a need for more robust emotional support systems.
